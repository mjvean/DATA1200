    Booking

    Welcome to Osloveien Event house, in central Oslo. We have a lot of experience with both big and small events, be it weddings, birthdays, seminars, conserts and more. Our venues can host events up to 3000 people! Depending on your needs we have two diffrent venues.

    We can deliver a full package of services at all times. Do you need food for you event? Do you need sound and lighting? Are you gone need an amphitheater for you seminar or you want your guests to sit at tables at your party? We have staff onboard that can handle anything and make you event run as smooth as possible without the need of your attention.

    Below you can see the venues we offer. If you are interested in booking one of our venue for your event, please fill in the form below and we will be in touch!

    Venue: Storsalen

    Storsalen can host events up to 3000 people, 1500 if seated. It has integrated sound system, a big screen and full lighting-rig. If you want details of the sound and lighting please contact us.


    Venue: Lillesalen

    Lillesalen can host events up to 1400 people, 700 if seated. It has integrated sound system, a big screen and a small lighting-rig. If you want details of the sound and lighting please contact us.
